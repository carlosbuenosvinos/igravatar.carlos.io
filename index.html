iGravatar Manager your Gravatar account
from your mobile

iGravatar is your mobile app for managing your Gravatar account. You will be able to upload new pictures from your iPhone or Android library or directly from your camera, assign pictures to your email addresses and so on.

If you use any of the webs that integrates with Gravatar, such as wordpress, github or hoosuite, you need this app. In the next release, you will be able to sync your iPhone contacts pictures with Gravatar.

What is Gravatar?
Your Gravatar is an image that follows you from site to site appearing beside your name when you do things like comment or post on a blog. Avatars help identify your posts on blogs and web forums, so why not on any site? Visit http://en.gravatar.com
